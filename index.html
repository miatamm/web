About Mia

I work as a freelance consultant helping companies get the most out of the Atlassian ecosystem. My role is not just about configuring Jira or Confluence — it’s about understanding how your teams work, what challenges you face, and then designing the right setup to support you.

Over the years I’ve partnered with SaaS vendors, consulting firms, and enterprises to:

  • Implement Jira and Confluence in a way that matches real-world processes
  • Select and integrate Marketplace apps that solve concrete problems
  • Create documentation and practices that make adoption easier and reduce support load

My approach is straightforward: listen first, design solutions that fit, and deliver quickly so your teams can move forward with confidence.
